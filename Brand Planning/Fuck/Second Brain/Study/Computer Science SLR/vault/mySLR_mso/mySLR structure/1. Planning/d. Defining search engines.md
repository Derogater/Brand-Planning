Defining search engines This definition depends on the area of systematic review. In general, considering the main electronic search engines that include the researches produced in the area of Computer Science, the revision should cover part (if not all) of the following bases: 
1. [IEEE Xplore](www.ieeexplore.com)  
2. [Scopus](www.scopus.com) 
3. [ScienceDirect](www.sciencedirect.com) 
4. [Springer](www.springerlink.com) 
5. [ACM](www.portal.acm.org) 
6. [Compendex](www.engineeringvillage.com)
